Security


Need to report a security vulnerability?

If you have found a security or privacy issue at LeanBoard, we ask you to share your insights and email us at security@leanboard.io

‍
We work hard in keeping LeanBoard safe for everyone. However it might occur that bad, unforseen things happen. We appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner. Please support us in making sure we understand the scope of the issue and address your concern. We take security disclosures serious and consider them our highest priority.

Please act in good faith towards our users’ privacy and data during your disclosure. LeanBoard requests that you don’t post or share any information about a potential vulnerability in any public setting until we have researched, responded to, and addressed the reported vulnerability. Also, we respectfully ask that you do not post or share any data belonging to our users.

Thank you for supporting LeanBoard!
