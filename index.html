Built for GitHub

Lean Board is built for GitHub. Simply login with your GitHub account and choose an Issue to connect your board. An uplinked snapshot of the connected board is being embedded in the issue's content so you can always stay up to date.

Why we built leanboard

We are a team of software developers and designers having worked together in real life before going remote. What we've missed most was our whiteboard and we couldn't find a simple solution that easily integrates into our workflow. That's why we build LeanBoard.
